# Guglex Technologies Launches Ewale: Everyday Payments in One Place

Today, Guglex Technologies is proud to announce the launch of **Ewale** — a consumer payments platform built for the everyday services Ghanaians actually use.

With Ewale, you can buy **WASSCE results**, **ECG prepaid**, **airtime**, and **data bundles** from one place — online at [ewalepay.com](https://ewalepay.com) or on any phone via USSD **\*714\*22#**. No app download. No juggling five different channels. Just fast, clear payments that work across major networks.

This launch is more than a product release. It is the result of years of building payment systems, integrations, and digital products for African markets — and deciding it was time to ship something people can use every day.

---

## Why We Built Ewale

Across Ghana, people already pay for essential digital services every week: topping up a phone, buying power units, checking exam results, or renewing data. The need is not new. The friction is.

Today, those purchases are often scattered:

- Different portals for different services
- Unclear success or failure states after payment
- Channels that work for one network but not another
- Experiences that assume everyone has a smartphone and stable data

We built Ewale to collapse that friction into a single, reliable product. Our goal was simple: **make essential purchases feel instant, trustworthy, and available to everyone** — not only users with the newest phones.

Ewale sits at the intersection of what Guglex has always done well: product engineering, payment infrastructure, and real-world usability.

---

## What You Can Do on Ewale Today

### 1. WASSCE results
Students, parents, and schools need a dependable way to purchase and access WASSCE results. Ewale streamlines that flow so the path from payment to confirmation is clear.

### 2. ECG prepaid
Buying electricity units should not feel like a multi-step negotiation with technology. Ewale supports ECG prepaid top-ups designed for speed and confirmation you can trust.

### 3. Airtime
Top up any major network without switching between apps or dealer menus. The experience is built for quick, repeat purchases.

### 4. Data bundles
Select and pay for data bundles across networks from the same place you buy airtime — so everyday connectivity stays simple.

Each of these is a high-frequency use case. Together, they make Ewale a practical daily utility, not a one-off novelty.

---

## Two Ways to Use Ewale

### On the web: [ewalepay.com](https://ewalepay.com)
A clean, mobile-first experience for users who prefer browsing, reviewing options, and paying online.

### On any phone: dial **\*714\*22#**
USSD remains one of the most inclusive digital channels in Africa. Feature phones, limited data, or unstable connectivity should not block someone from buying airtime or power. Ewale’s USSD access puts the product in reach of more people, on more devices, in more places.

That dual approach — web and USSD — is intentional. Digital products in Ghana succeed when they meet users where they already are.
